FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before joining. Can't find an answer? Just message us on WhatsApp.

Ask on WhatsApp →
How do I get paid for tasks?+
Once your task is submitted and verified by Hustl, payment is released directly to you — typically within a few hours. We use fast local transfer methods to make sure you get your money without friction.
Are the tasks from real businesses?+
Yes, 100%. Every business on Hustl is manually verified before they can post tasks. We do not allow fake or spam task submissions. Your time is valuable and we protect it.
Who can join Hustl as a student?+
Any Nigerian student with a smartphone can join. Whether you're in university, polytechnic, or a college of education — if you have basic internet access, you can earn on Hustl. No experience is required for basic tasks.
How fast are payments after task completion?+
Most task payments are processed within 2–6 hours of verification. Hustl's goal is same-day payment for all completed, verified tasks. We believe fast pay keeps hustlers motivated.
How much can I realistically earn per month?+
It depends on how many tasks you pick up. Students doing 3–5 tasks a day typically earn between ₦20,000 and ₦50,000 per month. More tasks = more income. There's no cap on what you can earn.
What kind of tasks are available?+
Tasks include social media engagement, sharing promotional posts, writing captions, collecting leads, content reposting, and online reviews. All tasks are simple, digital, and completable from your phone in under 90 minutes.
Is there a fee to join Hustl?+
No. Joining Hustl as a student is completely free. We earn when businesses pay for completed tasks. Students never pay any fee to access or complete tasks on the platform.
