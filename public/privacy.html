🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Last updated: May 2025 · Applies to all Hustl users in Nigeria
🔒

Our Commitment to You

We will never sell your personal data. Hustl collects only the information necessary to operate the platform and connect students with businesses. We do not share your information with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

1 What Information We Collect

When you sign up for Hustl, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your account type (student or business)
  • Your encrypted password (we never store your plain-text password)
  • The date you joined the platform

We do not currently collect payment details, phone numbers, or location data directly through the website.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your Hustl account
  • Connect you with relevant tasks or students (depending on your role)
  • Send you important platform updates via WhatsApp or email
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Improve the platform based on usage patterns

3 How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely on our backend server hosted on Railway. Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing (bcryptjs) before being stored — meaning even we cannot see your password.

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. Do not share your Hustl password with anyone.

4 Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited information in the following cases:

  • With businesses when you claim their task (your name may be visible to them)
  • With law enforcement if legally required to do so
  • With service providers who help us run the platform (e.g. hosting providers), under strict confidentiality

5 WhatsApp Communication

Hustl operates primarily through WhatsApp for task distribution and support. By joining our WhatsApp groups, you agree that your phone number and WhatsApp display name will be visible to group members. Please do not share sensitive personal information in WhatsApp groups.

6 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us on WhatsApp at +234 912 247 8160.

7 Cookies

Hustl does not currently use tracking cookies. We use browser localStorage to keep you logged in between sessions. This data stays on your device and is not sent to any third party.

8 Children's Privacy

Hustl is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform grows. Any significant changes will be communicated via WhatsApp or email. Continued use of Hustl after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10 Contact Us

For any privacy concerns or data requests, reach us on WhatsApp: +234 912 247 8160
